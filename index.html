Our Approach

01

Field First

Every project begins with going there. We prioritize direct engagement with local communities to understand their real needs.

02

Long-term Trust

We invest in relationships, not transactions. Sustainable partnerships with local organizations form the foundation of everything we do.

03

About Axis

25 years of going
where it matters.

Axis began in 2001 as the overseas dispatch team "Pax IV Hyper" within Keio University's Rover Scout chapter (慶應ローバー). For over two decades, we promoted international cooperation, social service, environmental conservation, and human development across Asia.

In 2024, we relaunched as an independent student organization — Axis — with clearer accountability and an expanded vision. The same commitment to direct field engagement. A new chapter.
